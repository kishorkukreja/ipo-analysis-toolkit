## Retail Investor Caveats

- This is a calendar and source-checking output, not an apply/avoid recommendation.
- SME IPOs can require higher minimum application amounts and may have lower post-listing liquidity than mainboard IPOs.
- Expected T+3 dates are not guarantees; official exchange, registrar, and issuer notices override computed dates.
- GMP, if mentioned from another source, is informal and unregulated and should not be treated as a guaranteed listing gain.
