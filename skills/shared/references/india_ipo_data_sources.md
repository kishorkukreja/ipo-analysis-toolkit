# India IPO Data Sources

## Preferred Official Sources

- SEBI: offer documents, observations, regulatory circulars.
- NSE/BSE: issue pages, subscription data, listing notices, anchor disclosures, SME platform notices.
- Registrars: KFin Technologies, MUFG Intime/Link Intime, Bigshare Services, Maashitla, and issue-specific registrar pages.
- Issuer and BRLM pages: RHP/prospectus links, price band ads, basis of allotment notices.

## Secondary Sources

- Chittorgarh, InvestorGain, IPOWatch, broker education pages, and reputable financial media can fill gaps.
- Label secondary-source fields clearly when official data is unavailable.
- Cross-check GMP and subscription from more than one source when practical.

## Source Caveats

- GMP is not official, not regulated, and can change rapidly.
- Aggregator calendars may lag official exchange updates.
- Subscription snapshots need a timestamp and category definitions.
- Allotment links and registrar forms vary by issue and may only activate after finalization.
