# Sources And Privacy

## Source Hierarchy

Use sources in this order:

1. SEBI circulars, SEBI investor education pages, official offer documents, NSE/BSE exchange pages, registrar pages, and SCSB/bank ASBA pages.
2. RHP/prospectus, issuer pages, BRLM notices, basis of allotment notices, and price band advertisements.
3. Broker education pages for app labels, mandate timing notes, and platform-specific operational details.
4. IPO aggregators and media only as secondary context.

If live issue data is missing, say exactly which fields are missing and ask for the official issue page, RHP/prospectus, registrar name, price band, lot size, category, and dates.

## Privacy And Fraud Safety

Every hands-on guide must include:

- Use only official broker, bank, exchange, SEBI, registrar, or issuer/BRLM links.
- Do not share UPI PIN, OTP, demat login, broker PIN, bank password, or full account credentials.
- Approve only mandates where issue name, amount, and linked bank account are correct.
- Use the applicant's own UPI ID and bank account.
- Treat third-party UPI IDs or bank accounts as invalid or high rejection risk.
- Do not pay money to anyone outside the ASBA/UPI mandate process.
- Avoid unofficial allotment pages that ask for unnecessary personal data.
