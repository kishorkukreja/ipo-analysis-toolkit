## Retail Investor Action

1. Confirm official issue details from RHP/prospectus, exchange page, broker issue page, bank ASBA page, or registrar.
2. Confirm PAN, active demat details, own UPI ID/bank account, and sufficient clear balance.
3. Submit the bid through the selected route.
4. Approve the UPI mandate or confirm the bank ASBA lien.
5. Save application/reference number.
6. Track allotment through the registrar first, exchange alternatives second, broker app third.

## Troubleshooting

| Symptom | What to check |
|---|---|
| No UPI mandate | UPI ID, broker status, issue cutoff, bank ASBA fallback |
| Mandate approved but broker pending | UPI mandate history, bank lien, status sync lag |
| Block still visible after non-allotment | Bank lien status, registrar status, SCSB unblock processing |
| Allotted but not in demat | T+2 schedule, active demat, DP/client ID, registrar/DP escalation |

## Safety

- Use only official broker, bank, exchange, SEBI, registrar, issuer, or BRLM links.
- Do not share UPI PIN, OTP, demat login, broker PIN, bank password, or full account credentials.
- Use the applicant's own UPI ID and bank account.
- Do not treat this as an apply/avoid recommendation or a guarantee of allotment/listing gains.
